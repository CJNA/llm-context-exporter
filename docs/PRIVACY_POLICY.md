# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 15, 2024

## Introduction

LLM Context Exporter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our software and services.

**Our Core Privacy Principle:** Your data belongs to you. We are designed to process your information locally on your device whenever possible, minimizing data collection and ensuring you maintain control over your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### CLI Tool (Command-Line Interface)

**Data Collection: NONE**

The CLI tool:
- ✅ Processes all data locally on your device
- ✅ Does not transmit any data to external servers
- ✅ Does not collect any personal information
- ✅ Does not track usage or analytics
- ✅ Does not require internet connectivity for core functionality

### Web Interface (Optional Hosted Service)

When you use our web interface, we may collect:

#### Information You Provide
- **Email Address**: Only if you choose to provide it for beta access or payment processing
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely by Stripe (we never see your credit card details)
- **Feedback**: Optional feedback you choose to provide about the service

#### Information Automatically Collected
- **Usage Statistics**: Number of exports, file sizes, target platforms (for beta users only)
- **Session Data**: Temporary data stored locally in your browser during use
- **Error Logs**: Technical error information to help us improve the service (no personal data)

#### Information We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Your ChatGPT conversation content
- ❌ Your extracted context or personal information
- ❌ Browsing history or behavior outside our service
- ❌ Device information beyond basic browser capabilities
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Social media profiles or connections

## Data Processing and Storage

### Local Processing First
- **Primary Processing**: All context extraction and formatting happens on your device
- **No Content Transmission**: Your ChatGPT conversations never leave your device
- **Temporary Storage**: Web interface stores files temporarily (automatically deleted after 1 hour)

### Data Storage Locations
- **User Data**: Stored securely in encrypted databases
- **Payment Data**: Handled entirely by Stripe (PCI DSS compliant)
- **Session Data**: Stored temporarily in your browser
- **Backups**: Encrypted and stored securely with limited access

### Data Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls limiting who can view data
- **Regular Audits**: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Secure Infrastructure**: Hosted on secure, monitored infrastructure

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Share Your Data
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described below:

#### Service Providers
- **Stripe**: For payment processing (they have their own privacy policy)
- **Hosting Providers**: For secure infrastructure (data is encrypted)

#### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Protect our rights and property
- Ensure user safety
- Investigate fraud or security issues

#### Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, user data may be transferred, but the new entity must honor this privacy policy.

## Data Retention

### Retention Periods
- **Session Data**: Automatically deleted after 1 hour
- **Beta User Data**: Retained during beta program, then deleted or anonymized
- **Payment Records**: Retained as required by law and Stripe's policies
- **Feedback**: Retained until no longer needed for product development

### Deletion Process
When you request data deletion:
1. We immediately stop processing your data
2. Data is securely deleted from active systems within 30 days
3. Backup data is deleted within 90 days
4. Some data may be retained longer if required by law
